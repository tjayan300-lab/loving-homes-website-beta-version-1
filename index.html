About Us

Loving Homes began as a small, family-run luxury dog retreat led by veterinary nurses and trainers who believed every stay should feel like home. Over the years, we’ve grown into a vet-guided haven that blends expert care with enrichment-first fun. Today, our team supports dogs of all ages and needs with compassion, safety, and joy.

  • Vet-led wellness oversight and 24/7 monitoring
  • Enrichment-first activities tailored to each dog
  • Transparent updates and easy owner check-ins
  • Safety-first design with climate-controlled comfort
